Tracking of a nav menu

How quickly do your users find their seeked category?

Tracking method

  • Track when a user hover the menu (long enough)
  • Lauch a timer
  • When a customer clicks on a category, stop the timer and send an event
  • The event's value is the number in ms taken before choosing the category
